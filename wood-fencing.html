Best Wood Types for Fencing in Huntsville, AL

Selecting the right wood type is essential for long-term durability in Alabama's climate. We use only premium materials that stand up to humidity, heat, and seasonal weather changes.

Western Red Cedar

Naturally resistant to rot, decay, and insects. Dimensional stability in humidity. Beautiful natural color that weathers to a elegant silver-gray. Takes stain and paint exceptionally well.

Premium Choice

Pressure-Treated Pine

Budget-friendly without sacrificing durability. Chemically treated to resist rot, termites, and fungal decay. Readily available and easy to work with. Holds paint and stain well.

Best Value

Wood with Steel Posts

Combines the beauty of wood with galvanized steel posts for maximum durability. Eliminates post-rot issues. Ideal for Huntsville's clay soil and wet conditions. Lifetime structural warranty.

Maintenance Tips for Wood Fencing in Huntsville, AL

Routine maintenance helps extend the lifespan of your wood fence and keeps it looking beautiful in Alabama's climate.

Stain or Seal

Apply stain or sealant every 3-5 years to protect against moisture and UV.

Clear Vegetation

Keep plants and debris away from the fence base to prevent rot.

Inspect Regularly

Check posts, boards, and hardware for signs of wear or damage.

Clean Gently

Wash with mild soap and water. Avoid pressure washing on high settings.
