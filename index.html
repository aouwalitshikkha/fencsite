Frequently Asked Questions

Fence costs vary by material, height, and linear footage. Wood fencing typically ranges from $15-$30 per foot, vinyl $25-$40, chain link $10-$20, and aluminum $20-$35. Contact us for a free, no-obligation estimate tailored to your project.
Most residential fence installations take 1-3 days depending on the size and complexity. We'll provide a timeline during your free estimate.
Permit requirements vary by city and county. In unincorporated Madison County, permits are typically required for fences over 6 feet. We handle the permitting process for you.
Wood privacy fences (6-8 ft tall) and vinyl privacy fences are the most popular choices for Huntsville homeowners. Both provide excellent privacy and can be customized to match your home.
Yes! We offer flexible financing options to help make your fence project affordable. Ask about our low monthly payment plans during your free estimate.
