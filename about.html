Our Story

Huntsville's Trusted Fence Experts Since 2004

Fencing Huntsville AL was founded with a simple mission: provide North Alabama homeowners and businesses with quality fencing at fair prices, backed by exceptional service. What started as a small family operation has grown into one of Huntsville and Madison County's most trusted fence companies.

Over the past 20+ years, we've installed thousands of fences across Huntsville, Madison, Athens, Decatur, and the surrounding communities. Our reputation is built on quality craftsmanship, honest communication, and a commitment to getting the job done right — on time and on budget.

We're proud to be a locally owned and operated business. When you choose us, you're supporting a local family and a team that genuinely cares about your satisfaction.
